Thinking

From AI Efficiency
to Human Sensitivity

In enterprise scenarios, I use AI to improve efficiency, accelerate design iteration, and connect design with implementation. But I also care about the other side of AI: anxiety, distraction, weakened judgment, and the lack of emotional interaction. My long-term design direction is to build AI experiences that are not only powerful, but also gentle, understandable, and human-centered.

在企业场景中，我使用 AI 提升效率、加速设计迭代，并连接设计与实现。但我也关注 AI 的另一面：焦虑、分心、判断力外包，以及情感化交互的缺失。我的长期设计方向，是创造不仅高效强大，也更温和、可理解、以人为中心的 AI 体验。

Efficiency

AI-native workflows for faster design iteration, from concept to interactive prototype.

System

Design systems and implementation constraints that bridge design intent with engineering reality.

Sensitivity

Gentle, human-centered experiences that respect attention, emotion, and wellbeing in the AI era.
